### Why This Challenge?

I launched the 365-day bug bounty challenge to:

1. **Master Bug Bounty Hunting**: Develop advanced skills in vulnerability identification and exploitation
2. **Build Consistency**: Prove that daily effort compounds into exceptional results
3. **Share Knowledge**: Document the journey to help aspiring security researchers
4. **Make Impact**: Discover and responsibly disclose vulnerabilities that matter
5. **Grow Community**: Connect with like-minded security professionals

### The Challenge Format

This challenge consists of:

- **365 Daily Logs** - Short-form entries documenting daily progress, findings, and learnings
- **52 Weekly Reviews** - In-depth posts analyzing the week, strategies, and reflections
- **Open Documentation** - All progress tracked transparently through this site

### Key Principles

🎯 **Consistency Over Intensity**  
Small daily efforts compound. I commit to 4 hours of focused work daily rather than sporadic marathon sessions.

📚 **Continuous Learning**  
Every vulnerability teaches something new. I document patterns and techniques to build deeper expertise.

🤝 **Responsible Disclosure**  
All findings are reported ethically through official bug bounty programs and coordinated disclosure processes.

🌱 **Community Contribution**  
This journey is shared openly. My learnings and methodologies are available to help others grow.
